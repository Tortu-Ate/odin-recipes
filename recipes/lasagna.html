Lasagna

Homemade Lasagna is a classic that every cook should have in their rotation.

Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce make the perfect dish!

While there are a few steps to this recipe, it‘s easy to make and has huge flavor. This dish can be made ahead of time and freezes well either before or after baking!

Ingredients

  • 1 (26 ounce) jar PregoÂ® Traditional Italian Sauce
  • 6 each uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • cup grated Parmesan cheese

Diretions

  1. Spread about 1 cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish (11x7-inch).
  2. Top with 3 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese and 1 cup pasta sauce.
  3. Top with remaining 3 uncooked noodles and remaining pasta sauce.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour Uncover and top with remaining mozzarella cheese. Let stand 5 minutes.
